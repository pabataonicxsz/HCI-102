Afternoon Snack


Takoyaki of Addie's Takoyaki and Milktea from VaniTea


There's something magical about a perfect afternoon snack, Addie's Takoyaki and Vanitea has something for you. Nestled in the heart of our community, Addie's Takoyaki has taken a creative twist on the traditional Japanese street food by introducing their mouth-watering Ham and Cheese Takoyaki. These delicious, bite-sized balls are filled with a harmonious blend of gooey cheese and savory ham, perfectly complemented by a crispy exterior. Each bite delivers a burst of flavor that keeps you coming back for more, making it an irresistible treat for any takoyaki lover.



Pairing perfectly with Addie's savory creation is a refreshing beverage from Vanitea, a local favorite known for its innovative and delectable milk tea offerings. My favorite flavor the cookies and Cream, wither its ice cream or milktea, thats why I choose their cookies and cream. Their Cookies and Cream Milk Tea is a standout choice, featuring a creamy blend of rich, velvety milk tea with crunchy cookie bits. This delightful drink is not only a treat for your taste buds but also a feast for the eyes, often topped with whipped cream and cookie crumbles for that extra indulgence. The balance of sweetness and the unique texture make it a perfect complement to the savory takoyaki.


Together, Addie's Ham and Cheese Takoyaki and Vanitea's Cookies and Cream Milk Tea create an unparalleled snack experience. Whether you're taking a break from work, catching up with friends, or simply treating yourself, this combo offers a delightful escape. The savory takoyaki warms your soul, while the refreshing milk tea cools you down, creating a harmonious blend of flavors and textures that is sure to satisfy your afternoon cravings. Visit these local gems today and indulge in the ultimate afternoon snack duo.
