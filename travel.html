Matampa Salay Misamis Oriental


A Day at the Little Baguio of Matampa, Salay


Nestled in the heart of Misamis Oriental, Philippines, lies a hidden gem waiting to be discovered by travelers seeking tranquility and serenity amidst nature's embrace. Welcome to Matampa Salay, a picturesque town renowned for its cool climate and stunning landscapes reminiscent of Baguio City, hence the name Little baguio of Salay Misamis Oriental. Matampa Salay is a haven for nature lovers, boasting lush greenery, rolling hills, and majestic pine trees that paint a mesmerizing backdrop against the clear blue skies. As you wander through its winding paths, you'll be greeted by the crisp mountain air and the soothing melody of birds chirping—a perfect symphony for relaxation and rejuvenation.



Matampa Pines or "Little Baguio" is 8.5 km from the National Highway in Salay, Misamis Oriental or 15 mins to 20 mins ride. It is accessible by cars, motocycle, and even mountain bikes. Its a 1 hour travel from Medina Misamis Oriental. The road is an uphill trip on a concrete road.


Matampa Salay, Misamis Oriental, is a hidden paradise waiting to be explored, offering a serene retreat amidst nature's bounty. Whether you're seeking a peaceful getaway or an adventure-filled escapade, this charming town has something to offer for every traveler. Come and discover the beauty of Matampa Salay—a cold oasis surrounded by pine trees, reminiscent of the enchanting charm of Baguio City.
